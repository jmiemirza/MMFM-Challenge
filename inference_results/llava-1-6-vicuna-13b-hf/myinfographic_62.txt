Badges and icons can serve multiple purposes on a website: - Establish trust: Endorsements from recognized organizations can reassure potential customers about the business's credibility. - Marketing: Displaying badges or logos can show partnerships with other notable brands, which may appeal to customers who are familiar with or loyal to these brands. - Authorization: Certain badges indicate certifications or accreditations for products, services, or standards that a business complies with. - User experience: A well-designed badge or icon can draw attention, providing users with quick insights into the business's affiliations or recognitions.
