The infographic mentions methods such as advertising, direct marketing, personal selling, and sales promotions, which are all designed to communicate the benefits and value of a product to potential consumers in order to persuade them to buy the product. Advertising involves reaching a wide audience through various media to generate awareness and interest in a product. Direct marketing involves reaching out to customers directly with promotional materials, such as flyers and brochures. Personal selling involves face-to-face selling by a salesperson to the potential customers. Sales promotions are short-term incentives offered to encourage customers to buy a product or service, such as discounts or giveaways.
